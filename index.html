Privacy Policy

Calculator Vault

Protecting your personal files while respecting your privacy.

Last Updated:

Calculator Vault is designed to protect your personal files while respecting your privacy. This app operates primarily offline, and we do not collect or transmit your personal data.

1. Information Collection and Use

Calculator Vault does not require account registration or personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

User Files (Photos, Videos, Documents)

When you choose to secure files, they are encrypted locally on your device using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. These files never leave your device and are not uploaded to any server.

Private Browser

The app includes a private browser feature for user convenience. This feature uses internet access to load web pages. The private browser does not log, store, or share browsing history, cookies, or user activity within the app. Any data entered on external websites is handled solely by those websites and not by Calculator Vault. However, third-party websites may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

2. Permissions and Their Purpose

Calculator Vault requests only the minimum permissions required:

  • Internet Access: Used exclusively for the private browser feature.
  • Notifications: Notifications are used to display user-visible progress updates for file encryption and restoration processes. No background or promotional notifications are sent.
  • Foreground Service: Foreground Service is used only during active user-initiated operations such as encrypting or decrypting files. A visible system notification is shown during these operations to comply with Android requirements.
  • File Access (via System Picker): The app uses Android’s Storage Access Framework (SAF), meaning it can only access files that you explicitly select. The app does not have access to all files on your device.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All encrypted files are stored within the app’s private storage area on your device.
  • Encryption: Files are secured using AES-256 encryption.
  • PIN Protection: Access to the vault is protected by a PIN. The PIN is never stored in plain text. A secure hashed version is stored locally for authentication.
  • Data Control: All data remains under your control. You can delete files at any time from within the app or by clearing app data.

4. Data Sharing

Calculator Vault does not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties.

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or remote data collection services.

5. Transparency

The vault feature is an intended and clearly disclosed functionality of the application. Users are informed within the app and its description that files can be securely stored and accessed using a PIN. The app does not hide functionality from users or reviewers. All features operate as documented, and no hidden or deceptive operations are performed.

6. No Background Data Collection

The app does not run background processes for data collection, tracking, or monitoring user activity. All operations are user-initiated and occur only when the app is actively in use.

7. Children's Privacy

Calculator Vault is not intended for children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can permanently delete all stored data by clearing app data or uninstalling the application.

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
