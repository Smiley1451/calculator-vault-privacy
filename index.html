Privacy Policy

Calculator Vault

Protecting your personal files while respecting your privacy.

Last Updated:

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application “Calculator Vault” developed by ProHands. Calculator Vault is designed to protect your personal files while respecting your privacy. This app operates primarily offline, and we do not collect or transmit your personal data.

1. Information Collection and Use

Calculator Vault does not require account registration or personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

User Files (Photos, Videos, Documents)

When you choose to secure files, they are encrypted locally on your device using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. These files never leave your device and are not uploaded to any server.

Private Browser

The private browser is designed to minimize data storage within the app. Calculator Vault does not intentionally store browsing history or user activity. However, external websites and underlying web components may store cookies or temporary data according to their own privacy policies.

2. Permissions and Their Purpose

Calculator Vault requests only the minimum permissions required:

  • Internet Access: Used exclusively for the private browser feature.
  • Notifications: Used only to display progress updates for encryption and restoration processes. No promotional or background notifications are sent.
  • Foreground Service: Used only during active, user-initiated operations such as encrypting or decrypting files, with a visible system notification.
  • File Access (via System Picker): Uses Android’s Storage Access Framework (SAF), meaning access is limited only to files you explicitly select.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All encrypted files are stored within the app’s private storage area on your device.
  • Encryption: Files are secured using AES-256 encryption.
  • PIN Protection: Access to the vault is protected by a PIN, stored only as a secure hashed value locally.
  • Data Control: You can delete your data at any time from within the app or by clearing app data.

4. Data Sharing

Calculator Vault does not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties.

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or remote data collection services.

5. Transparency

The vault feature is an intended and clearly disclosed functionality of the application. Users are informed within the app and its description that files can be securely stored and accessed using a PIN. The app does not hide functionality from users or reviewers. All features operate as documented, and no deceptive or hidden behavior is performed.

6. No Background Data Collection

The app does not run background processes for data collection, tracking, or monitoring user activity. All operations are user-initiated and occur only when the app is actively in use.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can permanently delete all stored data by clearing app data or uninstalling the application.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
